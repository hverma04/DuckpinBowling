What is Duckpin Bowling?

About Duckpin

Duckpin bowling is a classic New England-based variant that uses smaller balls without finger holes and lighter, squatter pins. Because it's a bit tougher to get a strike, bowlers get three rolls per frame instead of two. It's a fun, challenging, and highly authentic traditional bowling experience where a perfect 300 game has never been officially rolled!

Welcome to the Lanes

Whether you are a dedicated league bowler, a local regular, or a family looking for an energetic experience, our lanes are ready for you.

Preserving a Local Legacy

Established as Charlie Dinnigan Lanes around 1943, our building has been a neighborhood staple for decades. We are proud of our deep roots in the community.

We operate using original, ~60-year-old mechanical pinsetters. These machines require deep mechanical knowledge and custom repairs to keep running. When you bowl here, you aren't just playing a game—you are keeping a piece of history alive.
