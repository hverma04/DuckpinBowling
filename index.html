Welcome to Chelmsford Lanes.

Step through our doors whether you're a seasoned league bowler or bringing the kids for a weekend outing, there’s a lane waiting for you here.

Let's Roll

Bowling for Everyone

Duckpin uses smaller balls and lighter pins, making it the perfect game for all ages. No special gear required. Just bring your friends and have the time of your life!

Lane Rentals

Grab your friends and family for an authentic bowling experience. Walk-ins are always welcome, but giving us a call to reserve a lane guarantees your spot on the wood.

Parties & Events

Celebrate with us! From children's birthday parties to corporate team-building, our nostalgic lanes offer the perfect, welcoming backdrop for your next unforgettable gathering.

Pro Leagues

Looking for friendly, local competition? Join one of our long-standing duckpin leagues. Bowlers of all skill levels meet weekly for great games and great company.

A Neighborhood Staple Since 1943

Originally Charlie Dinnigan Lanes, we’ve been North Chelmsford's home for authentic duckpin bowling for over 80 years. We still operate on the original mechanical pinsetters keeping it vintage to respect our roots. When you step through our doors, you aren't just a customer. You're part of our local family.
