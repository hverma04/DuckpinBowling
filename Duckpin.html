What is Duckpin Bowling?

Duckpin bowling is a classic New England-based bowling variant. We aren't your average mega-entertainment complex; we offer a highly authentic, traditional bowling experience.


  • The Gear: We use smaller balls and pins.
  • The Rules: Because it's tougher to get a strike, you get three rolls per frame.
  • The Challenge: Higher difficulty means a perfect game is incredibly rare.

Welcome to the Lanes

Whether you are a dedicated league bowler, a local regular, or a family looking for a nostalgia-driven visit, our lanes are ready for you.

Preserving a Local Legacy

Established as Charlie Dinnigan Lanes around 1943, our building has been a neighborhood staple for decades. We are proud of our deep roots in the community.

We operate using original, ~60-year-old mechanical pinsetters. These machines require deep mechanical knowledge and custom repairs to keep running. When you bowl here, you aren't just playing a game—you are keeping a piece of history alive.
